About

This project will help to identify given species of plant using shape shape similarity of leaf. It can be helpful for botanist, student, farmers and others who are willing to get information about plant. It can help deaf and blind people to recognize plant species around them. Our system will work on the image provided by the user. It will extract the contour data of the leaf image and compare it to the valid information on the database. Hence it will provide the information about plant from the generated leaf data and valid data on database. It will also suggest similar plants that is similar to that leaf.
